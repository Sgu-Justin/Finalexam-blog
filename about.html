About Me

This is what I do.

Welcome to my corner of the web! I'm a 19-year-old powerhouse with a burning passion for powerlifting and a keen interest in the realms of AI and Data Science. Over the past 2 and a half years, my journey into the world of weightlifting has been nothing short of transformative, guided by the expertise of national champions in powerlifting who have shared their invaluable insights with me.

As a dedicated IT student immersed in the fascinating world of artificial intelligence and data science, I've found a unique balance between my academic pursuits and my love for lifting heavy. Juggling the demands of both the gym and the classroom requires discipline, but the lessons learned under the barbell translate seamlessly into the rigorous challenges of my academic endeavors. My blog serves as a beacon for those curious about embarking on their own weightlifting journey, offering a roadmap that merges the realms of powerlifting and technology.

Beyond the weights and algorithms, I also cherish my love life, proving that a passionate pursuit of fitness and intellectual growth can coexist harmoniously with personal connections. By prioritizing time management, setting clear goals, and maintaining a holistic approach to life, I've discovered the recipe for a fulfilling and balanced lifestyle. Join me on this adventure where the worlds of strength, intellect, and love converge, and let's redefine what it means to be a powerhouse in every aspect of life.
